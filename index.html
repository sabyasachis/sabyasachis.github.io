About me

I am a Research Associate in Machine Learning Lab in Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore advised by Prof. Chiranjib Bhattacharyya. I am also affiliated to ARTPARK as a Consultant.

I will be joining as a Ph.D. student at MILA and Université Laval in fall 2021 under Prof. Christian Gagné, MILA & Université Laval. I will also be co-advised by Prof. Frédéric Precioso, INRIA & Université Côte d’Azur.

My research interests include generative models, model explainability, medical imaging, LiDAR/3D computer vision and autonomous vehicles. I am currently working on unsupervised learning (generative models, disentanglement, domain adaptation), explainable models, AI for healthcare (disease classification/ segmentation) and robotics (LiDAR, SLAM). For more information, check out my Research Works or Explore by Keywords below.

I also regularly organize talks for ML/Autonomous Navigation Paper Reading Group ( youtube) and work part time as Research Mentor at SHARE Labs. Prior to this, I have worked on Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing research projects at Donut Research Labs and have worked on large software codebases at NVIDIA, Bangalore.
